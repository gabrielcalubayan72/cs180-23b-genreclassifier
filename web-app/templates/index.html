This project utilizes the Naive Bayes Classifier to analyze song lyrics and assign genres.

We aim to:

Aid in the preservation of Filipino culture in digitizing and archiving songs with genre metadata.

Assist songwriters by classifying their music's genre and providing context for artistic identity, community, and stylistic exploration.
